# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Applicants and reviewers using the workflow in a focused, task-oriented session.

Applicants need to create, edit, submit, and resume applications quickly without wondering what state they are in.
Reviewers need to find the right item, understand what changed, and approve, reject, or request changes with confidence.

## Product Purpose

This product manages an application submission and approval workflow with clear ownership, role-based actions, and an auditable history of status changes.

Success means the interface gets out of the way:

- users can see the next valid action immediately
- workflow state is always legible
- the reviewer path feels controlled and trustworthy
- the applicant path feels simple and fast

## Brand Personality

Quick, trustworthy, concise.

The voice should feel product-first rather than promotional: clear labels, direct guidance, and minimal narrative.
The UI should communicate competence through restraint, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- crypto app interfaces
- web3 app interfaces
- college project styling

Also avoid anything that feels overdesigned, playful without purpose, or like it is trying to prove it is clever.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize the next action. Every screen should make the current goal obvious.
- Use familiar product patterns. The interface should feel immediately usable to a person who already knows good SaaS tools.
- Keep copy short and operational. Prefer labels and instructions that help users act now.
- Make trust visible through clarity. State, role, and consequence should be easy to read.
- Keep the surface calm. Reserve emphasis for real workflow moments and errors, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No special requirements beyond WCAG AA and reduced motion.
